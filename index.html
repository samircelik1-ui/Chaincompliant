About us

A verification method built on transparency

ChainCompliant was founded to give individuals and businesses a clear way to assess the risk exposure of an address before a transaction or a relationship with an exchange.

Every request is cross-referenced with public sanction lists and on-chain analysis, and passes through human review before being returned: we don't generate instant verdicts, because real compliance requires time and context.

The report you receive explains what was checked, what emerged, and what the recommended next steps are.
